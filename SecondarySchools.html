SECONDARY SCHOOLS

Journey Continue: Secondary Schools

2013 was the year I set foot in the realm of high school. A significant difference can be felt when at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Pendamaran Jaya because students who are not from their primary school rarely get the opportunity to stand out in sports. In 2014, I moved to a boarding school, Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Batu Kikir, here I learned the meaning of independence and don't easily trust people. I once again moved school in mid -2016 because I was dropped from the hostel for some reason. At Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Rantau Panjang, Klang, I took SPM and finished my schooling.

S.M.K Batu Kikir Logo

The finest achievement in S.M.K Batu Kikir: Representing schools at the national level in creative video creation competitions under the central zone school category. Obtained result 5A1B1C1D1F for Form 3 Examination (PT3).

S.M.K Rantau Panjang

The finest achievement in S.M.K Rantau Panjang: Represented schools at the district level for bowling competitions and placed 4th out of 16 schools competing. Took the Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia (SPM) examination and obtained a 5B4C result.
